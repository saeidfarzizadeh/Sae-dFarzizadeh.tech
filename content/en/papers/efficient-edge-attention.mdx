## Abstract

We present a hardware-aware compression pipeline that reduces transformer latency on embedded accelerators while preserving task accuracy within 1.2% of the baseline.

## Key contributions

1. Layer-wise sensitivity profiling tied to real power traces
2. Mixed-precision distillation schedule for attention blocks
3. Open benchmark suite for sub-5W inference targets

## Results

Our method achieves 3.8× speedup on ESP32-class devices with negligible accuracy loss on industrial anomaly detection tasks.
