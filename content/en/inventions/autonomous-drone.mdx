## Overview

Confined industrial spaces — tanks, ducts, crawl spaces — are dangerous for human inspectors and challenging for generic drones. This platform combines visual-inertial odometry with a domain-specific defect detector.

## Key Design Decisions

- **Protected rotor geometry** for wall proximity flight
- **Onboard ONNX inference** to avoid video uplink latency
- **ROS2 lifecycle nodes** for predictable startup in field kits

<Callout type="warning">
  GPS-denied navigation requires continuous VIO recalibration when lighting changes abruptly.
</Callout>

## Outcomes

Deployed in two pilot facilities with a 40% reduction in inspection downtime compared to manual rope-access methods.
